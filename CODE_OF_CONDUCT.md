Contributor Covenant Code of Conduct (Short)

Our standards

We expect contributors to maintain a welcoming, respectful, and harassment-free environment. Unacceptable behavior includes harassment, discrimination, and abusive language.

Reporting

If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, please report it by opening an issue at:

https://github.com/dheeraz101/Thoughts/issues

Be sure to mark the issue with a clear title such as "Code of Conduct report" and include relevant context. Maintainers will treat reports confidentially.

Enforcement

Maintainers may remove content or ban users who violate this code of conduct. In serious cases, incidents may be escalated to platform hosts.
